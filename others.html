Doc in Progress

I organize, in collaboration with Federico Fallucca and Enrico Savi, the periodic seminars of "Doc in Progress" at the Department of Mathematics of University of Trento. As the name suggests, the speakers are chosen from among currently PhD students in Mathematics or related fields.
For further information, visit the web page of the project here: Doc in Progress.
